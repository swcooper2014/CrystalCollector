The Chrystal Game

How To Play The Game:

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game bymatching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from your until you click on it.
  5. Each time when teh game started, the game will change the values of each crystal.
